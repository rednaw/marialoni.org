TbiLLC 2025: Workshop on Implicatures

About: The workshop is as part of the 15th International Tbilisi Symposium on Language, Logic and Computation (TbILLC 2025). It

brings together researchers who are working on implicatures and other pragmatic inferences from an experimental or theoretical perspective.

The program of the workshop will consist of presentations by invited speakers.

  • Jacopo Romoli Univeristy of Düsseldorf
  • Camelia Bleotu University of Bucarest
  • Fabian Schlotterbeck University of Tübingen
