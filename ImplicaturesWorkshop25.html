TbiLLC 2025: Workshop on Implicatures

The workshop, part of the 15th International Tbilisi Symposium on Language, Logic and Computation (TbILLC 2025), brings together researchers who are working on implicatures and other pragmatic inferences from an experimental or theoretical perspective.

The program consists of presentations by invited speakers.

  • Jacopo Romoli University of Düsseldorf
  • Camelia Bleotu University of Bucarest
  • Fabian Schlotterbeck University of Tübingen
